How We Work

01

Select Item for Rental

Click on one of the categories of items in which you want to rent or rather search an Item from the search bar at the top.
02

Reserve the Booking

After selecting an item from the categories. Reserve the item by filling in the reserve for booking form on the site.
03

Confirm with Vendor

Later on you will receive an email or text message from the vendor to confirm your booking and then you can now proceed to payment.

OUR SERVICES

Bajeti Rentals is a system which offers rental related services for tenants, vendors and marketers. This System is ideal for:

  • Tenants willing to rent products from Vendors in their locations
  • Vendors who have products in which they are willing to rent to tenants in their location
  • Marketers who are willing to advertise products of vendors to client thereby receiving a commission from the vendor per referal.
